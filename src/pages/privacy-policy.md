**Last updated: 27 April 2026**

---

The **Bangladeshi Students Society in Vienna (BSSV)** is committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the **EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR – Regulation (EU) 2016/679)** and the **Austrian Data Protection Act (Datenschutzgesetz – DSG 2018)**.

## 2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to personal data processed in connection with:

- Membership in the **BSSV WhatsApp group(s)**

## 3. Data Collected When Joining the WhatsApp Group

When you join the BSSV WhatsApp group, the following personal data may be processed:

| Data | Source | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| Name (as set in WhatsApp) | WhatsApp profile | Member identification within the community |
| Phone number | WhatsApp platform | Required to add you to the group |
| Profile picture | WhatsApp profile | Displayed to other group members |
| Messages sent in the group | Your activity | Community communication and information sharing |
| Status updates (if visible) | WhatsApp profile | Incidental visibility to group members |

> **Note:** BSSV administrators do **not** collect, store, or export your data outside of WhatsApp. However, all data visible inside the WhatsApp group is accessible to all group members (currently **students and community members in Vienna**). Group members may be located in Austria or other EU/EEA countries.

---

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

All personal data is processed on the following legal bases under **Article 6 GDPR**:

- **Art. 6(1)(a) – Consent:** By voluntarily joining the BSSV WhatsApp group via the invitation link, you give your explicit, freely given consent to have your profile name, phone number, and profile picture visible to other group members.
- **Art. 6(1)(f) – Legitimate Interests:** Maintaining a functional, safe, and informative community group for Bangladeshi students in Vienna constitutes a legitimate interest, provided it does not override your fundamental rights and freedoms.

You may **withdraw your consent at any time** by leaving the WhatsApp group. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal.

---

## 5. Purpose of Data Processing

Your personal data is processed exclusively for the following purposes:

- **Community communication** – enabling members to connect, ask questions, and share information relevant to student life in Vienna
- **Information sharing** – distributing announcements, guides, and updates about student life, visa procedures, accommodation, and events in Vienna
- **Group administration** – managing membership, maintaining a respectful environment, and enforcing community guidelines
- **Support and assistance** – helping newly arrived Bangladeshi students integrate into life in Vienna

BSSV does **not** use your data for commercial purposes, advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

## 10. Your Rights Under GDPR and Austrian DSG

As a data subject, you have the following rights under GDPR (Articles 15–22) and the Austrian DSG:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR)** | Request a copy of the personal data BSSV holds about you |
| **Right to Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)** | Request correction of inaccurate personal data |
| **Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (Art. 17 GDPR)** | Request deletion of your personal data where no overriding legitimate purpose exists |
| **Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18 GDPR)** | Request that we limit how we use your data |
| **Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR)** | Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format |
| **Right to Object (Art. 21 GDPR)** | Object to processing based on legitimate interests |
| **Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR)** | Withdraw consent at any time (e.g., by leaving the WhatsApp group) |

To exercise any of these rights, contact the BSSV group administrators via the WhatsApp group.

We will respond within **30 days** as required by GDPR.

## 12. Group Governance and Democratic Structure

BSSV operates as a **flat, democratic community**. The following governance rules apply to all members and administrators:

### 12.1 Equal Standing of All Members
No admin holds special authority over any member beyond the technical functions granted by the WhatsApp platform. All group decisions, changes, and actions are made by **majority voting only**. This reflects the principle of **equal treatment** under **Article 20 of the EU Charter of Fundamental Rights** and the democratic values upheld by Austrian civil association law (**Vereinsgesetz 2002, BGBl. I Nr. 66/2002**).

### 12.2 Protection Against Arbitrary Removal
Once a member has joined the group and meets all entry requirements, **no admin or member has the right to remove them without a majority group vote**. Any removal without due process would constitute a violation of the member's right to fair treatment and community participation. This is grounded in:
- **Article 47 of the EU Charter of Fundamental Rights** – right to an effective remedy and fair process
- **Austrian Vereinsgesetz 2002, § 13** – rules on exclusion of members from associations must follow fair procedures

By **joining or remaining** in the BSSV WhatsApp group, every member explicitly agrees to:

- **EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR – Regulation (EU) 2016/679)**
- **Austrian Data Protection Act (Datenschutzgesetz – DSG 2018)**

### 13.1 Prohibition on Sharing Member Data
Sharing any member's **personal data, phone number, profile picture, screenshots, or contact details** outside this group without their **explicit written consent** is strictly prohibited under:
- **Art. 5(1)(b) GDPR** – purpose limitation: data shared within the group may only be used for the purposes of that group
- **Art. 5(1)(f) GDPR** – integrity and confidentiality: personal data must be protected against unauthorised disclosure
- **Art. 83(4) GDPR** – violations may result in administrative fines of up to **€10,000,000** or 2% of total worldwide turnover
- **§ 63 DSG 2018** – unauthorised processing or disclosure of personal data is a criminal offence under Austrian law, punishable by fines

> Violating this rule may expose the responsible member to **civil liability** under Austrian law (**ABGB § 1293 ff. – damages**) and potential **criminal prosecution** under **§ 118a StGB** (unauthorised access to data) and **§ 51 DSG 2018**.

This group exists **exclusively to support and connect Bangladeshi students in Vienna**. All members are expected to maintain a **respectful, helpful, and welcoming environment** for everyone.

This purpose is protected under:
- **Art. 11 EU Charter of Fundamental Rights** – freedom of expression and information

## 17. Community Guidelines Summary

All BSSV WhatsApp group members are responsible for respecting the privacy and dignity of fellow members:

- **Do not share** messages, contact details, or personal information of other group members outside the group without their explicit consent

- **Respect all democratic decisions** made by group vote

Violation of these guidelines may result in immediate removal from the BSSV community and, where applicable, legal action.
