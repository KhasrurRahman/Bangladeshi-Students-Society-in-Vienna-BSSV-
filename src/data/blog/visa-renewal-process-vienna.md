## 📅 Appointment Booking

To renew your student residence permit, you need to **book an appointment with MA35**.  
➡️ [Click here to book your appointment](https://www.wien.gv.at/kontakt/ma35-terminbuchung-verlaengerungsantraege). You can book at any MA35 branch, regardless of your district of residence.

---

## 📄 Required Documents

1. **Application Form (Antrag):**

   - ➡️ [German Form (DE)](https://www.bmi.gv.at/312/60a/files/B/029_2025_Antragsformular_B_Aufenthaltstitel_Drittstaatsangehoerige_DE_V20250717_bf.pdf)
   - ➡️ [English Form (EN)](https://www.bmi.gv.at/312_EN/60a/files/B/029_2025_Antragsformular_B_Aufenthaltstitel_Drittstaatsangehoerige_EN_V20250717_bf.pdf)

2. **Passport & Photos:**

   - Original passport and a copy
   - One recent biometric color photo (size 3.5 × 4.5 cm), no more than 6 months old

3. **Accommodation Proof:**

   - Valid accommodation contract
   - Recent proof of rent payments, such as bank statements
   - **Meldezettel** (official address registration)

4. **Proof of Funds (2026):**
   For a standard 12-month renewal, you must show the following base amount:

   - Student under 24: **€722.58/month**, or **€8,670.96 for 12 months**
   - Student aged 24 or older: **€1,308.39/month**, or **€15,700.68 for 12 months**
   - Married couple: **€2,064.12/month in total**, or **€24,769.44 for 12 months**
   - Each child: **an additional €201.88/month**, or **€2,422.56 for 12 months**

   These figures come from the [OeAD 2026 student residence permit checklist](https://oead.at/en/to-austria/entry-and-residence/residence-permit-student-no-mobility-programme) and the [BMI guide to calculating financial means](https://www.bmi.gv.at/312/files/broschuere_unterhaltsberechnung_publikation_a4_2026_converted_webbf.pdf).

   These are base amounts. If your accommodation costs more than **€386.43/month**, add the difference for each month. You must also add the cost of health insurance if you pay for it separately. For example, if your rent is €600/month, add €213.57/month, or €2,562.84 for 12 months.

   If you work legally, MA35 can consider your regular net salary as part of your financial proof. Submit your AMS employment permit, employment contract, your last 3 payslips where available, and bank statements showing the salary payments. You must still cover any remaining shortfall. There is no official €500/month exemption, and €3,000 to €5,000 is not a universal "safe" bank balance.

   Your savings should be in an account in your name that you can access from Austria. MA35 may also ask for bank statements from the last 3 months and evidence showing where the money came from.However, that scenario is the worst-case scenario.

5. **Health Insurance:**  
   Valid Austrian health insurance (ÖGK or private). If you are eligible and not insured through another route, [ÖGK student self-insurance](https://www.oesterreich.gv.at/de/themen/bildung_und_ausbildung/hochschulen/universitaet/4/1/Seite.160809) costs **€78.84/month in 2026**.

6. **Study Documents (combine all in one folder):**

   - Studienbestätigung (Enrollment certificate)
   - Studienbuchblatt (Latest academic record)
   - Proof of academic progress (8 semester hours or 16 ECTS per academic year)

7. **Financial/Legal Report:**
   - Current KSV self-disclosure: [Request the free Article 15 GDPR report](https://digitalerantrag.ksv.at/Dip/?product=auskunft-nach-art-15-dsgvo). [MA35 confirms](https://www.wien.gv.at/amtswege/aufenthaltsbewilligung-student) that it accepts this free version.

## Costs to budget for (2026)

- **Residence permit application fee:** €218 for each permit. The fee is paid when you apply and is not refunded if the application is refused or withdrawn. A combined renewal and change-of-purpose application costs more. See the [City of Vienna's official student residence permit page](https://www.wien.gv.at/amtswege/aufenthaltsbewilligung-student).
- **Student health insurance:** €78.84/month, or €946.08 for 12 months, if you use the reduced ÖGK student self-insurance and meet its conditions.
- **KSV self-disclosure:** Free when you request the Article 15 GDPR report linked above.
- **Other possible costs:** Biometric photos, certified translations, apostilles, and document legalisation. These prices depend on the provider and the documents you need.
