For visa renewal, individuals are usually asked to submit a **KSV1870 report**, which normally costs **€48** to obtain. But there's a free way: the Austrian credit-check company **KSV1870** lets you request all personal data they hold on you under **GDPR Article 15** — completely **free of charge**. Anyone can use this method instead of paying the €48 fee.

---

## ✅ What You Get

✔️ All personal data KSV1870 has stored under your name
✔️ A **PDF report** (sent as a download link)
✔️ Completely **free** — no payment required

---

## 📝 Steps to Apply

1️⃣ Go to the official request page:
🔗 [Art. 15 GDPR Request Form (KSV1870)](https://digitalerantrag.ksv.at/Dip/?request=auskunft-nach-art-15-dsgvo&redirect=auskunft-nach-art-15-dsgvo)

2️⃣ Fill in your name, address, and date of birth, then click **"Request information now"**

3️⃣ Wait for processing (average **10 days**, legally up to 1 month)

4️⃣ You'll receive a PDF download link by email — it stays active for **14 days**, so download it in time

---

## ⚠️ Things to Keep in Mind

- Requests are processed **strictly in the order received** — there's no way to jump the queue
- The name and address you enter must match your Meldezettel, or verification may be delayed
- This is not the "official" KSV score certificate used for bank loans or rentals — it's simply your own stored data under GDPR

---

1. Open the link and fill out the form
2. Wait about 10 days
3. Download the PDF from the emailed link (within 14 days)
